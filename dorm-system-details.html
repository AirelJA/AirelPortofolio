Dormitory System Information

Dormitory System Information is a web-based application designed to streamline the management of boarding house operations. This platform provides tools for managing room availability, tenant information, room types, and facilities, making it easier for owners to monitor and organize their business. The system includes features for customer management, payment tracking, and communication, enabling efficient handling of tenant inquiries and transactions. With a user-friendly dashboard displaying key metrics, Sistem Informasi Kost helps boarding house owners oversee their operations effectively and deliver a better experience for tenants.
